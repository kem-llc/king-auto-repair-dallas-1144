Our story

Dallas's Trusted Auto Repair Shop Since 2008

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our customers safe and satisfied, building lasting relationships through transparency and expertise.

King Auto Repair was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: provide Dallas drivers with reliable, honest auto repair at fair prices. Owner Mike King, an ASE Master Technician with over 20 years of experience, saw too many customers being overcharged or misled by other shops. He set out to create a different kind of repair shop—one where integrity comes first.

Over the years, King Auto Repair has grown from a small two-bay garage to a full-service facility serving thousands of satisfied customers. We've invested in the latest diagnostic technology and ongoing training for our team, but our core values remain the same: treat every customer like family and never recommend a repair that isn't needed.
