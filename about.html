Our story

Dallas's Trusted Auto Repair Shop Since 2009

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe on the road. We believe in transparency, craftsmanship, and treating every customer like family.

King Auto Repair was founded in 2009 by master mechanic John King with a simple vision: create an auto shop where customers receive fair prices and expert work without the hassle. Starting with just two bays in a small Dallas garage, John's commitment to integrity and quality quickly earned a loyal following. Word spread, and the shop expanded to serve drivers across the city.

Today, King Auto Repair operates a state-of-the-art facility with five bays and a team of ASE-certified technicians. We continue to invest in the latest diagnostic tools and training to service all makes and models. Despite our growth, we maintain the same customer-first values that started it all: honesty, quality, and respect.
