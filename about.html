Our story

Your Neighborhood Auto Repair Experts

At King Auto Repair, our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep Dallas drivers safe and mobile. We believe in transparency, fairness, and building lasting relationships with our customers.

King Auto Repair was founded in 2010 with a simple vision: create an auto shop where customers are treated like family. Owner Marcus King, a master ASE-certified technician, wanted to bring his decades of experience to a shop that prioritized quality over profit. Starting with just two bays and a handful of loyal customers, the shop grew through word-of-mouth referrals thanks to its reputation for honest diagnostics and fair pricing.

Today, King Auto Repair operates a full-service facility on Buckner Boulevard, serving drivers across Dallas, Mesquite, and Garland. Our team of certified technicians continues Marcus's legacy of integrity, using the latest tools and techniques to keep your car running at its best. We're proud to be a trusted part of the community.
