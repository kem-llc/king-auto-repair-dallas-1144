Common questions

How often should I replace my brake pads?

Typically every 30,000 to 70,000 miles depending on driving habits and vehicle type. We recommend an inspection at every oil change.

What are signs my brakes need service?

Squealing or grinding noises, a pulsating brake pedal, longer stopping distances, or a warning light on your dashboard.
