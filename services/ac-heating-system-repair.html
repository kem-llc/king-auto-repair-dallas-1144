AC & Heating System Repair

Stay comfortable year-round with expert AC and heating diagnostics and repairs.

Schedule this service

Overview

A malfunctioning AC or heating system can make driving miserable. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair all climate control issues, from refrigerant leaks to faulty compressors. We restore your system to peak performance so you stay cool in summer and warm in winter.

What's included

  • Fast, accurate diagnosis of AC and heating problems
  • Complete system service including recharge and leak repair
  • Comfort you can count on in any weather

How it works

1

Step 1: System Inspection

We check refrigerant levels, compressor function, and airflow to identify the issue.

2

Step 2: Leak Detection & Repair

Using UV dye and electronic detectors, we find and fix any leaks in the system.

3

Step 3: Recharge & Test

We recharge refrigerant to factory specs and test the system to ensure it's blowing cold (or hot) as expected.

Common questions

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

The most common cause is low refrigerant due to a leak. Our diagnostic service will pinpoint the leak and repair it before recharging.

How much does an AC repair cost?

Costs vary depending on the issue. Simple recharges start around $150, while compressor replacements can be more. We always provide an estimate upfront.
